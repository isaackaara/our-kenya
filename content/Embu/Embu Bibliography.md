# Embu Bibliography

Major academic and journalistic sources addressing Embu history, culture, and contemporary issues. This bibliography provides entry points for further research on the Embu people.

## Foundational Academic Sources

https://www.jstor.org/stable/3054233 - Scholarly studies of Mount Kenya cultural complex including Embu ethnic identity and social organization

https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/abs/10.1080/03056244.2015.1005633 - Academic articles addressing Embu history, land, and contemporary issues

https://www.britannica.com/place/Kenya - General Kenya historical and geographical information

https://www.britannica.com/topic/Kikuyu-people - Comparative information on related Mount Kenya communities

## Government and Official Sources

https://embu.go.ke/ - Embu County government official website with governance, development, and administrative information

https://www.knbs.or.ke/ - Kenya National Bureau of Statistics providing census and national data

## International Development and Research

https://www.fao.org/countries/570/en/ken/ - Food and Agriculture Organization Kenya country information including agricultural production data

https://www.who.int/countries/ken/ - World Health Organization Kenya health statistics and information

https://www.unep.org/explore-topics/water - United Nations Environment Programme addressing water and environmental issues

## News and Journalism

https://www.standardmedia.co.ke/counties/embu - Standard Media coverage of Embu County news and current affairs

https://www.bbc.com/news/world-africa-15932886 - BBC coverage of Mau Mau historical issues

## Human Rights and Governance

https://www.hrw.org/world-report/2024/country-chapters/kenya - Human Rights Watch Kenya human rights reporting

https://www.amnesty.org/en/location/africa/east-africa/kenya/ - Amnesty International Kenya human rights documentation

https://www.ipu.org/parline/reports/structure/KE - Inter-Parliamentary Union information on Kenya's parliament and representation

## Land and Natural Resources
