# Kenyan Somali

Kenyan Somali communities constitute a distinct ethnic and cultural group with historical ties to the Horn of Africa.

Kenyan Somali experienced marginalization during the Moi era and security challenges from cross-border conflicts.

Somali communities maintain strong cultural connections and face persistent security and development challenges.
