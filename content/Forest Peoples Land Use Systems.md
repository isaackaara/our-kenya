# Forest Peoples Land Use Systems

A methodological guide to Forest Peoples Land Use Systems, providing frameworks for understanding Kenyan history. This period witnessed significant developments that shaped Kenya's historical trajectory, with forest peoples land use systems serving as an important focal point.

The context of forest peoples land use systems illuminates how local, regional, and national scales of historical change intersected and reinforced each other over time. Understanding this topic provides crucial perspective on Kenya's broader historical development and contemporary institutions.
