# Nairobi Urban Identity

Nairobi's rapid urbanization has created distinctive cosmopolitan identity transcending ethnic ties.

Nairobi hosts diverse ethnic communities living in shared urban spaces. Urban anonymity and economic opportunity enable cross-ethnic associations.

Nairobi also demonstrates residential segregation, inequality, and informal settlement challenges.
