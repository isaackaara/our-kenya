The [[Kikuyu]] people's educational prominence emerged from colonial preferential investment in central highlands and post-independence institutional concentration. Kikuyu communities' early embrace of education, [[Kikuyu Independent Schools|independent schools movement]], and geographic proximity to Nairobi created educational advantage. Elite institutions like [[Alliance High School Elite|Alliance High School]] concentrated in Kikuyu regions, creating pathway for regional elite capture of educational opportunity. The concentration of educated Kikuyu in government, business, and professional positions reflected both historical opportunity and educational advantages.
