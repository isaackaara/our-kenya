Daniel arap Moi's presidency (1978-2002) accelerated university expansion and implemented the 8-4-4 education system (1985). Unlike Kenyatta's restrictions, Moi rapidly expanded higher education access, establishing multiple public universities. The 8-4-4 system explicitly prioritized practical skills and self-reliance aligned with Moi's Harambee philosophy. Moi emphasized education's role in nation-building through mandatory national service and patriotic curricula. Education also experienced increasing political control and restrictions on academic freedom during his tenure.
