# Kenya Lionesses: Women's Rugby Team and World Series Growth

Kenya's women's rugby team (Lionesses) competes in the World Rugby Women's Sevens Series and international rugby competitions. Women's rugby in Kenya has emerged from minimal participation to developing competitive programs. The Lionesses have participated in World Series events and gained international exposure, representing Kenya's growth in women's sports participation.
