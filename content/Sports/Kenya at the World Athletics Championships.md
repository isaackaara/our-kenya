# Kenya at the World Athletics Championships: Complete Record

Kenya has competed in every World Athletics Championships (held biennially since 1983) and has achieved medals at every edition, demonstrating consistent excellence. Kenya's medal count at World Championships totals approximately 80-90 medals, with roughly 40-50 gold medals, making Kenya one of the most successful nations in World Championships history.

Kenya's dominance in distance events at World Championships mirrors its Olympic success. The World Championships represents Kenya's most important international athletic competition and typically receives significant national focus.
