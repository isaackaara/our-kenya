# Kenya 1984 Los Angeles Olympics: Return After Two Boycotts

Kenya's 1984 Los Angeles Olympics appearance marked Kenya's return to Olympic competition after boycotting both 1976 (Montreal) and 1980 (Moscow). Kenya competed at Los Angeles without major boycott participation and achieved medals. This Games represented the resumption of normal Olympic participation for Kenya after the previous two boycotts had disrupted the competition cycle.
