# Harambee Stars History: Kenya's Football National Team

Harambee Stars is Kenya's national football team, competing in FIFA World Cup qualifiers and Africa Cup of Nations tournaments. The team's history spans from Kenya's independence era through the present, marked by inconsistent performance and occasional highlights but generally unsuccessful tournament records.

Kenya hosted the Africa Cup of Nations in 2004, reaching the tournament's semi-finals but ultimately delivering disappointing results at home. Kenya has never won the AFCON and has rarely advanced far in the tournament despite periodic competitive performances.

The national team's underperformance reflects broader governance and development issues in Kenyan football rather than lack of talent or player quality.
