Swimming in Kenya faces significant challenges due to limited pool infrastructure and warm climate conditions that do not favor natural water-based training. Few Kenyans compete internationally at elite swimming levels. Kenya's geography (tropical/subtropical) and lack of developed swimming facilities create barriers to developing competitive swimming programs compared to nations with extensive pool networks.

Kenya's swimming sector represents underutilized sporting potential and infrastructure gap.
