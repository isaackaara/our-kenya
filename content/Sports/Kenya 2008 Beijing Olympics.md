Kenya's 2008 Beijing Olympics appearance included a historic breakthrough: [[Pamela Jelimo|Pamela Jelimo]] won gold in the women's 800 meters, becoming the first Kenyan woman to win an Olympic athletics gold medal. Additionally, Samuel Wanjiru won the marathon gold medal, representing Kenya's continued dominance in distance running across both men and women.

These victories were significant because they broadened Kenya's Olympic success beyond male distance runners and demonstrated Kenya's emerging capability in women's athletics.
