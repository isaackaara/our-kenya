# Kenya Hockey: Field Hockey and Asian Community Sport

Field hockey in Kenya has roots in British colonial administration and Indian expatriate communities. Kenya's hockey teams compete at international level in African championships. Hockey remains a minority sport in Kenya, primarily participated in by urban communities and schools with access to facilities. Kenya's hockey international record demonstrates capability but limited dominance compared to other sports.
