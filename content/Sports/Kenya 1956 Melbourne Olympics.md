# Kenya 1956 Melbourne Olympics: First Olympic Appearance

Kenya competed in the 1956 Melbourne Olympics for the first time, representing the nation on the international Olympic stage. Kenya did not win any medals at these Games, establishing a beginning point for Kenya's Olympic journey. The 1956 Olympics represented Kenya's entry into Olympic competition, preceding the historic success of later Games by over a decade.
