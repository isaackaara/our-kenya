# Safari Rally Golden Era: 1970s-1990s as WRC Round

The Safari Rally's peak as a World Rally Championship round occurred from the 1970s through 1990s. During this era, the rally attracted world champion drivers and top WRC teams. The grueling Kenya terrain, wildlife encounters, and unpredictable conditions provided tests unlike European-based WRC rallies. Multiple world champions won the Safari Rally, confirming its prestige and difficulty.

The Safari Rally represented Kenya's contribution to international motorsport at the highest level.
