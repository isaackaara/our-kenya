# 2004 AFCON Kenya: Hosting Africa Cup of Nations

Kenya hosted the 2004 Africa Cup of Nations football tournament. This represented the highest profile international football tournament hosted in Kenya and showcased Kenya's football infrastructure and passion. However, Harambee Stars' performance was disappointing, failing to advance far in the tournament despite playing at home. Kenya's relative underperformance in a home tournament reflected the gap between fan support and actual team quality.
