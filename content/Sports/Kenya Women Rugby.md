# Kenya Women Rugby: Lionesses and World Series Performances

Kenya's women's rugby team (Lionesses) competes in the World Rugby Women's Sevens Series and has achieved competitive performances on the international stage. Women's rugby in Kenya has grown from minimal participation to developing programs that compete regularly at world level. The Lionesses' performances demonstrate Kenya's capability in women's sports and represent growth in women's athletic opportunities.
