# Kenya Premier League: Domestic Football Competition

Kenya's top domestic football league features clubs including Gor Mahia, AFC Leopards, and others competing for the championship title. The league's structure, format, and financial stability have varied over decades. While Kenya's football league exists and attracts domestic support, it has not developed the investment levels or commercial infrastructure of leading African football leagues.
