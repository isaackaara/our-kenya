# Kenya Sport Future: Prospects for 2030 and Beyond

Kenya's sporting future depends on addressing governance failures while building on existing athletic dominance. Distance running success appears sustainable given Kenya's altitude training infrastructure, established talent pipeline, and global market demand for marathon competition. Women's athletics represents a growth area where Kenya has demonstrated rapid emergence as a global power.

However, challenges persist: anti-doping enforcement requires sustained international oversight, governance failures in football and other sports require institutional reform, and youth development infrastructure needs investment. Kenya's sports future will be shaped by whether governance improvements match its athletic talent.
