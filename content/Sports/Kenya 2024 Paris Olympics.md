# Kenya 2024 Paris Olympics: Faith Kipyegon's Three Golds

Kenya's 2024 Paris Olympics appearance featured [[Faith Kipyegon|Faith Kipyegon]]'s remarkable third consecutive Olympic 1500m gold medal, a historic achievement making her one of the few athletes with three golds in the same event. Kenya continued its tradition of Olympic distance running success, demonstrating sustained excellence into the 2020s.
