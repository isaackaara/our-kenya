# Sports Funding Kenya: Government Fund and Patronage Dynamics

Kenya's government allocates funding to sports through the Sports Fund and Ministry of Sports. This funding is intended to support athlete development, facilities, and sporting administration. However, allocation of government sports funding has been marked by patronage dynamics, with funds often flowing to administrators and politically connected individuals rather than directly supporting athletes or facilities.

The lack of transparent allocation mechanisms for sports funding has contributed to governance failures across Kenyan sports, from athletics to football. Athletes often lack direct government support and rely on private agents, sponsors, and prize money from competitions.
