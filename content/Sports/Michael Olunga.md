# Michael Olunga: Kenya's Prolific Striker and All-Time Leading Scorer

Michael Olunga is Kenya's all-time leading goal scorer in international football and has competed as a striker in Japan, Qatar, and other international leagues. His goal-scoring record represents Kenya's greatest individual football achievement at international level.

Olunga's success in Asian football leagues and his prolific scoring for Kenya's national team have made him one of Kenya's most recognizable footballers globally and a model for young Kenyan football talent.
