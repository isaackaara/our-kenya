AFC Leopards, originally Abaluhya FC, represents the [[Luhya|Luhya]] community in Kenya's football landscape. The club is Gor Mahia's primary rival and represents Kenya's major club football rivalry. AFC Leopards has won Kenya's top league multiple times and represents community identity and pride in western Kenya.

Gor Mahia versus AFC Leopards matches represent Kenya's most intense club football rivalry, with significant cultural and community dimensions beyond sport.
