# Football Kenya Corruption: FIFA Interventions and Repeated Scandals

The Football Kenya Federation has experienced multiple corruption scandals, including financial mismanagement, election disputes, and governance failures similar to those at Athletics Kenya. FIFA has suspended Kenya from international competition multiple times due to governance breaches. These interventions have disrupted Kenya's football program and prevented the national team from competing in major tournaments.

The repeated FKF corruption scandals reflect systemic governance weakness across Kenyan sports federations.
