# Rugby Growth Kenya: From Colonial Elite to National Sport

Rugby in Kenya emerged from British colonial administration and was initially an elite, urban sport. Through the late 20th century, rugby expanded from colonial schools and Nairobi's expatriate communities to broader Kenyan participation. Kenya's rugby development, particularly in sevens format, has transformed rugby from a niche sport to a legitimate area of Kenyan international sporting achievement.

Rugby's growth contrasts with football's dominance and demonstrates Kenya's multi-sport capability.
