# Kenya 2016 Rio Olympics: Multi-Generational Excellence

Kenya's 2016 Rio Olympics appearance demonstrated sustained excellence across athletics. [[Eliud Kipchoge|Eliud Kipchoge]] won the marathon gold medal, [[Faith Kipyegon|Faith Kipyegon]] won the 1500m gold medal, and [[Julius Yego|Julius Yego]] won silver in the javelin. This combination of achievements, including Kenya's first female 1500m Olympic champion and a field event medalist, demonstrated Kenya's athletic breadth.
