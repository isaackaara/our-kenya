Basketball in Kenya has grown from minimal participation to developing domestic leagues and international competition. Kenya's basketball league provides competitive opportunities, though participation remains significantly below football or athletics. NBA players of Kenyan heritage have gained international prominence, and Kenya has developed some international competitors, though basketball remains a minority sport in Kenya.

Kenya's basketball growth reflects global basketball expansion and investment by NBA organizations in African markets.
