# Kenya Sevens History: From Emergence to Global Powerhouse

Kenya's sevens rugby team emerged as a competitive force in the 1990s and reached its peak in the 2016-2019 period. During this era, Kenya regularly finished in the top four of the World Rugby Sevens Series and achieved medals at Commonwealth Games and other international competitions. Post-COVID (2020+), Kenya has experienced some decline in competitive performance, though the team remains competitive.

Kenya's sevens history demonstrates successful development from emergent nation to world-class competitor across a 25-year span.
