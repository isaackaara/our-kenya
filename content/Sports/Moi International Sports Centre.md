The Moi International Sports Centre, located in Kasarani district of Nairobi, is Kenya's primary international sports stadium. Built for the 1987 All Africa Games, the facility has hosted Kenya's major sporting events including athletics championships, football matches, and international competitions.

The stadium's capacity and facilities enable Kenya to host international sports events. However, the facility's maintenance has been inconsistent, and it has not been upgraded significantly since the 1980s, limiting its capability compared to modern international stadiums.
