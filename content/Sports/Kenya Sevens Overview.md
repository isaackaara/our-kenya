# Kenya Sevens Overview: Rugby Powerhouse

Kenya became a global rugby sevens powerhouse in the 1990s and 2000s, competing regularly in the World Rugby Sevens Series (now renamed HSBC World Rugby Sevens Series). Kenya's sevens team demonstrated exceptional capability in this fast-paced, shortened rugby format and achieved multiple medal finishes at Commonwealth Games and World Rugby Sevens Series events.

Kenya's dominance in sevens rugby reflected the sport's appeal in communities where rugby had become established, particularly in Nairobi and other urban centers. Unlike football (a sport at which Kenya underperforms), rugby sevens became an area of genuine Kenyan sporting excellence at the international level.
