# Court Records from Colonial Period

A methodological guide to Court Records from Colonial Period, providing frameworks for understanding Kenyan history. This period witnessed significant developments that shaped Kenya's historical trajectory, with court records from colonial period serving as an important focal point.

The context of court records from colonial period illuminates how local, regional, and national scales of historical change intersected and reinforced each other over time. Understanding this topic provides crucial perspective on Kenya's broader historical development and contemporary institutions.
