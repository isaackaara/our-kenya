# Understanding Customary Law

A methodological guide to Understanding Customary Law, providing frameworks for understanding Kenyan history. This period witnessed significant developments that shaped Kenya's historical trajectory, with understanding customary law serving as an important focal point.

The context of understanding customary law illuminates how local, regional, and national scales of historical change intersected and reinforced each other over time. Understanding this topic provides crucial perspective on Kenya's broader historical development and contemporary institutions.
