# Diaspora Communities

Kenyan diaspora communities abroad maintain economic, cultural, and familial ties to Kenya.

Remittances from diaspora constitute significant income for many families. Diaspora members engage in business, professional, and investment activities in Kenya.

Diaspora engagement in politics and civil society increasingly shapes Kenya's development discourse.
