# The Maasai Mara Ecosystem

The Maasai Mara ecosystem encompasses the interconnected natural systems supporting the exceptional wildlife concentrations and ecological productivity of the Maasai Mara region.

## Habitats

- Open grasslands: vast, treeless plains
- Acacia woodlands: scattered trees with grassy understory
- Riverine forests: dense vegetation along Mara and Talek rivers
- Escarpments and hills

## Hydrology

The Mara River and Talek River provide critical permanent water sources. Rainfall varies spatially across the ecosystem, with western highlands receiving more precipitation than eastern plains.

## Seasonal Dynamics

Rainfall drives vegetation patterns and animal movements. Short and long rains produce flush of grass growth, triggering herbivore migrations and predator concentrations.
