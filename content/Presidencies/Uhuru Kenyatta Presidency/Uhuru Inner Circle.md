---
title: "Uhuru Inner Circle"
---

Uhuru Kenyatta's inner circle combined family members, loyalists from his Kiambu political base, and strategic allies from across Kenya. His first term saw William Ruto as Deputy President and close ally, though their relationship soured by the second term. David Murathe emerged as a key political strategist and informal advisor. Cabinet Secretaries Fred Matiang'i and Joe Mucheru wielded significant influence, with Matiang'i effectively serving as a super minister coordinating government operations. Uhuru's relatives, including his brother Muhoho Kenyatta and nephew Jomo Gecaga, advised on business and political matters. After the 2018 Handshake with Raila Odinga, the inner circle expanded to include ODM figures. Wealthy businessmen with close ties to State House influenced policy, particularly around infrastructure and trade. The inner circle managed succession politics, the BBI process, and attempts to influence the 2022 election outcome.
