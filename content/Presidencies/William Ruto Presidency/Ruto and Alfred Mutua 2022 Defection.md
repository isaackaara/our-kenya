Alfred Mutua, Akamba politician and former presidential aspirant, defected from Azimio coalition to Kenya Kwanza with Mudavadi, strengthening Ruto's coalition by adding Ukambani community support. Mutua's defection signaled that Azimio was fragmenting weeks before election, suggesting Ruto was likely victor and politicians were positioning accordingly. Mutua's appointment to cabinet position validated his defection calculation. The pattern illustrated how elite defections operated: politicians bet on winners, switched for advantage, and expected compensation. Mutua's crossing meant Ruto could claim support beyond Kikuyu-Kalenjin axis, appearing broader-based despite remaining Kalenjin-dominated.
