---
title: "Ruto Inner Circle"
---

William Ruto's inner circle consists of politicians, technocrats, and business allies who supported him during his break with Uhuru Kenyatta and through the 2022 election. Key figures include Rigathi Gachagua (Deputy President until his 2024 impeachment), Moses Kuria, Kimani Ichung'wah, and Kindiki Kithure (who replaced Gachagua). Farouk Kibet, Ruto's longtime personal assistant, wields significant informal power. Caleb Kositany and Oscar Sudi are close political allies from the Rift Valley. Technocrats like David Ndii (economic advisor) and former Central Bank Governor Patrick Njoroge influenced policy, though Njoroge left amid disagreements. The Ruto network includes wealthy individuals who funded his campaigns, expecting returns through government contracts. The inner circle manages his hustler narrative, economic policy, and relationships with international partners. After Gachagua's impeachment, the composition shifted to include more opposition allies co opted into government.
