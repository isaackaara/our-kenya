Ruto's administration continued Kenya's renewable energy transition (geothermal, wind, solar), attempting to reduce electricity costs for industry and households. Yet transitions faced implementation delays, private sector resistance, and financing constraints. Energy sector remained critical bottleneck for manufacturing competitiveness and industrial development.
