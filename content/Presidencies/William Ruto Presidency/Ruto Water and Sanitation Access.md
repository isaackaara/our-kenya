Ruto emphasized water and sanitation expansion as development priority, continuing multi-decade challenge of universal access to clean water and sanitation. Early initiatives focused on dam construction (completing Arror/Kimwarer projects started under Uhuru) and pipeline expansion. Yet rural areas remained chronically underserved, with implementation lagging political promises. Water sector remained critical yet persistently underfunded relative to needs.
