Ruto's charitable foundation engaged in community projects (schools, water, health facilities) in Rift Valley, presenting himself as development-focused philanthropist. The foundation provided patronage distribution mechanism and poverty alleviation image. Yet foundation activities remained limited relative to government budgets and official development agencies, suggesting charity was supplementary rather than transformative.
