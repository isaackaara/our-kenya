Ruto's education agenda emphasized digital learning and technology integration in schools, attempting to modernize curriculum beyond traditional teaching methods. Early initiatives included laptops-for-schools programs and digital content development. Yet resource constraints limited scale, with most rural schools lacking electricity for digital devices. The technology agenda reflected aspirational governance exceeding implementation capacity.
