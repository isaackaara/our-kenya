# Eugene Wamalwa

Eugene Wamalwa is a contemporary Luhya politician who has held significant political office under multiple presidents, demonstrating the continued capacity of Luhya leaders to access high national positions despite their failure to win the presidency in recent decades.

## Parliamentary Career

Wamalwa was first elected Member of Parliament for Saboti constituency in 2007, beginning a parliamentary career that continues to the present day. His election coincided with the major constitutional and electoral reforms of the post-2007 crisis period.

## Cabinet Service Under Uhuru Kenyatta

Following the 2015 elections, President Uhuru Kenyatta appointed Wamalwa as Cabinet Secretary for the Ministry of Water and Irrigation (2015-2018). In this role, Wamalwa managed water resource management, irrigation development, and water supply for Kenya's population.

In 2018, Wamalwa was moved to the Cabinet position of Cabinet Secretary for Devolution and ASAL (Arid and Semi-Arid Lands) Areas, responsibilities involving coordination between the national and county governments and addressing challenges of arid-zone development.

Under President Uhuru Kenyatta, Wamalwa also served as Cabinet Secretary for Defense, a sensitive security position. His appointments across multiple portfolios demonstrate his recognition as a capable administrator by the Kenyatta administration.

## Known for Political Pragmatism

Wamalwa is noted for his close relationship with President Kenyatta dating back before Kenyatta's presidency. This personal connection facilitated his rapid political advancement within the Kenyatta administration.

## Contemporary Status

Following the 2022 elections, Wamalwa has continued political life, though his specific ministerial appointments or roles in the Ruto administration have evolved. His long parliamentary tenure and multiple cabinet positions have established him as a senior Luhya politician with substantial administrative experience.
