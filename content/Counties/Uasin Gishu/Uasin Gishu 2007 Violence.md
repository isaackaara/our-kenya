# Uasin Gishu 2007 Violence

[[../Uasin Gishu/Uasin Gishu County|Uasin Gishu County]] was one of the worst-affected areas of Kenya's 2007-2008 post-election violence. The violence was primarily directed at [[../../Kikuyu/Kikuyu|Kikuyu]] and [[../../Luo/Luo|Luo]] settler communities by [[../../Kalenjin/Kalenjin|Kalenjin]] mobs.
