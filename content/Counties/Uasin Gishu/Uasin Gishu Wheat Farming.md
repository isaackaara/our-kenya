# Uasin Gishu Wheat Farming

The broad Uasin Gishu plateau is Kenya's main wheat-growing area, producing the majority of Kenya's wheat supply. The plateau's high altitude, cool climate, moderate rainfall, and volcanic soils create ideal conditions for wheat cultivation.
