# William Ruto and Uasin Gishu

[[../../Kalenjin/William Ruto|William Ruto]] represents Uasin Gishu's Sugoi constituency and became Kenya's President in 2022. Ruto's political trajectory has made Uasin Gishu one of Kenya's most politically important counties.
