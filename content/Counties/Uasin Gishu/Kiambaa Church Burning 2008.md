# Kiambaa Church Burning 2008

On January 1, 2008, post-election violence led to the burning of the Assemblies of God church in Kiambaa village. A mob attacked the church where approximately 250 [[../../Kikuyu/Kikuyu|Kikuyu]] had taken shelter, killing approximately 35 people.
