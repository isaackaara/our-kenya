# Afrikaner Trek Uasin Gishu

In 1908, approximately 47 Afrikaner families, primarily from South Africa, trekked to settle the Uasin Gishu plateau in Kenya. The Trek was motivated by political developments in South Africa following the Anglo-Boer War and British colonial consolidation.
