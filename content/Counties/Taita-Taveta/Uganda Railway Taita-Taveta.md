# Uganda Railway Taita-Taveta

The Uganda Railway (completed 1901) passes through Taita-Taveta County, with Voi serving as the major junction where coastal and inland routes diverge. The railway was fundamental to colonial development, enabling transport of goods, people, and military forces. Today, the Standard Gauge Railway (SGR) operates on the same route, providing modern transport infrastructure.
