# Taita Hills Biodiversity

The Taita Hills are a global biodiversity hotspot with over 600 plant species (180 endemic), 300+ bird species, and numerous endemic amphibians, reptiles, and mammals. The isolated highland ecosystem has fostered speciation and high endemism, making it a conservation priority.
