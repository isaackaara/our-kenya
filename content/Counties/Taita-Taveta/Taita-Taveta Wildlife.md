# Taita-Taveta Wildlife

Wildlife in Taita-Taveta County encompasses the megafauna of Tsavo (elephants, lions, rhinos) and the endemic species of the Taita Hills. The county is a major wildlife conservation region with dual emphasis on large predator conservation and endemic species protection.
