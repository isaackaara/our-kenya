Kisumu envisions itself as "Kisumu City of Choice," a transformed regional center offering quality services, economic opportunity, and attractive living conditions. The vision encompasses lakefront development, improved infrastructure, and enhanced regional integration.

## City of Choice Vision

The "Kisumu City of Choice" initiative aims to position Kisumu as an attractive destination for investment, tourism, and residence. The vision includes improved governance, infrastructure, environmental quality, and service delivery.

## Lakefront Development

Lakefront development is central to Kisumu's future plans. The vision includes improved public access to the lake, restoration of degraded shorelines, development of waterfront parks and recreational facilities, and tourism development along the lakeshore.

## Port Development

Kisumu port revival is part of the development vision, with potential for expanded trade, improved infrastructure, and regional commercial growth. Port development would support fishing industry, regional trade, and economic growth.

## Infrastructure Investment

Future development requires substantial infrastructure investment including road improvement, water supply expansion, electricity distribution upgrade, waste management improvements, and telecommunications advancement.

## Economic Diversification

Development plans include economic diversification beyond fishing and agriculture, including manufacturing expansion, technology sector development, and tourism growth. However, investment and entrepreneurial initiatives are required.

## Tourism Development

Kisumu's lakefront location, cultural heritage, and regional significance offer tourism potential. Tourism development could generate employment and foreign exchange.

## Regional Integration

Kisumu's future depends substantially on successful East African Community integration. Regional trade growth and improved regional connectivity would support Kisumu's economic growth.

## Governance and Institutional Strengthening

Effective governance, institutional capacity, reduced corruption, and service delivery improvement are essential for development. County government strengthening is a priority.

## Environmental Restoration

[[../../Conservation/Lake Victoria Kenya|Lake Victoria]] water quality restoration, shoreline rehabilitation, and ecosystem improvement would support fishing industries, tourism, and public health.

## Housing and Settlement

Planned development includes housing construction, informal settlement upgrading, and organized urban expansion. Affordable housing provision is a key need.

## Education and Healthcare

Investment in education and healthcare quality and infrastructure is essential for human capital development and service delivery improvement.

## Financing and Investment

Realizing Kisumu's development vision requires substantial financial investment from government, private sector, and international sources. Mobilizing this investment is a central challenge.
