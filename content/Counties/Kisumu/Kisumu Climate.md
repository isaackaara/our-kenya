Kisumu's climate is hot and humid year-round, characteristic of tropical lakeside locations. The city's location on [[Lake Victoria Kenya|Lake Victoria]] creates a warm, humid environment with substantial annual rainfall.

## Temperature

Kisumu experiences consistently high temperatures throughout the year, with average temperatures ranging from approximately 20 degrees Celsius (68 Fahrenheit) at night to 28-32 degrees Celsius (82-90 Fahrenheit) during the day. Daily temperature variation is modest.

## Humidity

Humidity is characteristically high due to Kisumu's lakeside location. Humidity levels often exceed 70 percent, creating sticky, uncomfortable conditions, particularly during the warmest hours.

## Rainfall Patterns

Kisumu receives substantial rainfall distributed across two seasons: the long rains (March-May) and the short rains (October-December). Annual rainfall is approximately 1,500 to 2,000 millimeters (59 to 79 inches).

## Seasonal Variation

Seasons are primarily distinguished by rainfall patterns rather than temperature variation. The rainy seasons bring cloudier weather and reduced sunshine. The dry seasons (June-September and January-February) are sunnier and slightly cooler.

## Lake Victoria Influence

[[Lake Victoria Kenya|Lake Victoria]] moderates climate and influences local weather patterns. The large water body affects wind patterns, moisture, and local rainfall.

## Malaria Risk

The warm, humid climate and standing water near the lake create ideal conditions for mosquito breeding. Malaria is endemic and remains a significant health concern, particularly during and after rainy seasons.

## Heat Stress

The consistent heat and humidity create heat stress challenges, particularly for physical laborers. Dehydration and heat-related illness are concerns.

## Agricultural Impact

The rainfall and humidity support agricultural production, particularly in surrounding areas. However, flooding during heavy rains can damage crops and infrastructure.

## Climate Change Impacts

Climate change is altering Kisumu's rainfall patterns, creating uncertainty in water availability for agriculture and domestic use. Changing patterns affect farming livelihoods.

## Seasonal Living

Kisumu residents adapt their activities to seasonal patterns, with adjustments to clothing, work schedules, and social activities depending on rainfall and temperature.
