# Lake Turkana

World's largest alkaline lake, UNESCO World Heritage Site in Turkana County, addressing the unique challenges and opportunities of this remote northeastern Kenyan region.

## Context in Turkana

Lake  represents a crucial element of Turkana County's development landscape. The county faces distinctive challenges related to its position as a tri-border region where Kenya, Somalia, and Ethiopia converge.

## Key Aspects

The development of lake turkana in Turkana reflects broader patterns in Kenya's arid and semi-arid regions, with specific manifestations in this historically marginalized county.

## Contemporary Status

As of 2026, lake turkana remains a significant concern and development priority in Turkana County, with ongoing efforts to address challenges and build resilience.
