# Bomet NGOs

Non-governmental organizations (NGOs) and development organizations provide services and support community development in health, agriculture, education, and governance across Bomet County.

## Development Focus Areas

NGO activities include:
- Health service provision
- Agricultural development support
- Education programs
- Gender equality advocacy
- Water and sanitation projects
- Environmental conservation
- Governance and accountability
- Livelihood support programs

## Service Delivery

NGOs provide:
- Healthcare services
- Healthcare education
- Agricultural training
- Educational support
- Water supply projects
- Market access facilitation
- Advocacy and rights protection

## Capacity Building

Organizations support:
- Community training programs
- Farmer field schools
- Cooperative strengthening
- Leadership development
- Skills training initiatives
- Technical assistance

## Partnership Roles

NGOs work with:
- County government
- Community organizations
- Farmer groups
- Religious institutions
- International partners
- Donor organizations

## Sectoral Organization

NGOs organize around:
- Agricultural development
- Health improvement
- Education quality
- Women's empowerment
- Environmental protection
- Governance strengthening
- Youth development

## Funding Sources

NGO financing from:
- International donors
- Foundation grants
- Government contracts
- Community contribution
- Corporate partnerships
- Individual donations

## Community Impact

Organizations contribute:
- Service delivery gap filling
- Community mobilization
- Knowledge transfer
- Advocacy for marginalized groups
- Innovation demonstration
- Accountability monitoring
