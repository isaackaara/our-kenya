This timeline documents key dates and events in West Pokot County history from the colonial period through 2026.

## Early Colonial Period (1895-1920s)
1895: European explorers document western Kenya region including Pokot territory
1902: British colonial administration extended to Pokot territory
1905-1920: Colonial administrative posts established in Pokot area
1910: Kapenguria established as colonial administrative center

## Colonial Development (1920s-1950s)
1920-1945: Colonial administration of pastoral Pokot territory
1930s: Administrative structures formalized
1945: Post-World War II colonial reforms
1952-1960: Mau Mau Rebellion period, affecting colonial administration
1952: Jomo Kenyatta trial and detention at Kapenguria (1952-1961)

## Independence and Early Post-Colonial Period (1963-1980s)
1963: Kenya independence, Pokot territory incorporated in national state
1963-1973: Integration of pastoral regions into national administration
1970s: County administrative structures established
1978: National policy integration attempts expand
1980s: Pastoral raiding conflicts increase in frequency and lethality

## Modern Development (1990s-2000s)
1990s: Market liberalization affecting pastoral economies
1997: County administrative divisions reorganized
2000: Pastoral conflict escalation with armed raiding
2003: Livestock disease and drought crises affect pastoral populations
2005: Inter-communal pastoral conflict intensity increases

## Conflict and Intervention Period (2000s)
2006: Small arms proliferation increases pastoral conflict lethality
2007: Government security operations attempt to suppress raiding
2008: Disarmament programs implemented
2008-2010: Conflict reduction and stabilization efforts progress

## Devolution and Contemporary Period (2010-2026)
2010: Constitution review process nearing completion
2013: Devolution implemented, West Pokot County Government established
2013: First county elections, devolved administration begins
2015: County elections, administrative structure refinement
2017: National election cycle, regional political changes
2020: Third county election, continued devolved governance
2022: National and county elections, gubernatorial races
2022-2026: Climate adaptation and livelihood diversification initiatives
2026: Contemporary period of sustainable pastoral development planning
