Women in West Pokot County play important but often underrecognized roles in pastoral and agricultural production. Women manage households and care responsibilities while engaging in pastoral production activities. Women participate in milk marketing, generating household income from dairy products. Women engage in water and firewood collection, requiring substantial daily labor investment. Traditional decision-making authority in pastoral societies was primarily male, though women's voices increasingly influence household decisions. Women's education levels are lower than men's due to cultural preferences and limited school access. Women face barriers to land ownership, with customary inheritance patterns often favoring male heirs. Marriage practices affect women's rights and autonomous decision-making capacity.

## Economic and Social Roles
Women engage in small-scale trading (agricultural products, milk, consumer goods) providing household income. Women's participation in agricultural production increases in highland zones. Vegetable gardening provides household nutrition and income. Women manage household food security, controlling food preparation and consumption decisions. Women establish savings groups and rotating savings schemes (merry-go-round) for financial security. Water collection remains a significant female labor burden, affecting time available for other activities. Fuel collection for cooking energy involves women's labor. Animal husbandry decision-making increasingly includes women's input. Women's participation in community groups provides social connection and collective action platforms.

## Empowerment and Political Participation
Women's representation in county government has increased through elected and nominated positions. Gender quotas ensure women's involvement in governance. Women's empowerment programs promote economic independence and leadership. Women's groups engage in community development projects including water systems and school buildings. Girls' education campaigns aim to increase female school enrollment. Early marriage prevention initiatives protect girls' education access. Women's health services including maternal healthcare support women's wellbeing. Domestic violence against women persists, with limited formal support services. Women's land rights advocacy seeks to improve property ownership capacity. Women's participation in peace and conflict resolution initiatives has increased recognition of women's peacemaking roles.
