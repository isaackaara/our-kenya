Several prominent Kenyans have originated from or are closely associated with Nandi County, with many having achieved national and international prominence in politics, business, athletics, and other fields. Distance runners form the most visible group of notable Nandi figures, with numerous Olympic medalists, World Championship winners, and international-class runners from the county. These athletes have brought global attention to Nandi and created aspirational pathways for youth.

Political figures from Nandi have included members of parliament, cabinet ministers, and county governors who have shaped national and regional policy. Businesspeople with Nandi origins have established various enterprises including trading companies, sports coaching firms, and commercial operations. Academic and intellectual figures have contributed to Kenyan scholarship and public discourse. Cultural figures including musicians and artists have drawn on Nandi heritage in their work. The prominence of Nandi-origin individuals in national affairs reflects the county's distinctive history and characteristics. Athletics achievements have overshadowed other forms of accomplishment in contemporary Nandi, though Nandi continues producing leaders in diverse fields. International runners and coaches associated with Nandi have become ambassadors for the county and region. Historical figures including Koitalel arap Samoei remain important in cultural memory and identity.
