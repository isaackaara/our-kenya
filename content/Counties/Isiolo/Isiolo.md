# Isiolo

Isiolo is a county in northern Kenya, serving as a gateway between central Kenya and the arid northern frontier. The town of Isiolo is administratively significant as the headquarters of the former Eastern Region.

## Geography

Isiolo lies in the transition zone between Kenya's central highlands and the arid northern plains. The Ewaso Nyiro River flows through the region, providing critical water resources for pastoral communities.

## Communities

Multiple pastoral and agro-pastoral groups inhabit Isiolo:
- Samburu: Pastoral communities with ties to central Kenya
- Borana: Pastoral groups with Ethiopian connections
- Rendille: Primarily camel herders
- Meru: Agricultural communities
- Somali: Pastoral and trading communities

## Economy

- Pastoral livelihoods (cattle, camels, goats)
- Limited agriculture in riverine zones
- Wildlife tourism (Shaba National Reserve, Samburu)
- Regional trade hub

## Challenges

- Climate variability and recurrent droughts
- Inter-communal pastoral conflicts
- Limited economic diversification
- Infrastructure constraints
