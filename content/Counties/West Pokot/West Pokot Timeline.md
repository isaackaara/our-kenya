# West Pokot Timeline

1895: European explorers document western Kenya
1902: Colonial administration extended
1952-1961: Jomo Kenyatta detention at Kapenguria
1963: Kenya independence
2013: Devolution implemented, West Pokot County Government established
2026: Contemporary development planning
