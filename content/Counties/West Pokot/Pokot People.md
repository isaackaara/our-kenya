# Pokot People

[[../../Kalenjin/Kalenjin|Kalenjin]] (also called Suk) are a Kalenjin sub-group, see [[../../Kalenjin/Kalenjin.md]]. They are the dominant community in West Pokot County with distinctive pastoral and agro-pastoral culture. The Pokot maintain strong age-set systems and pastoral traditions.
