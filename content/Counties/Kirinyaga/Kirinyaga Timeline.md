# Kirinyaga Timeline

This timeline documents major events and developments in Kirinyaga County's history through 2026.

## Pre-Colonial Period
- Traditional [[../../Kikuyu/Kikuyu|Kikuyu]] settlement and land use on [[../../Conservation/Mount Kenya National Park|Mount Kenya]] slopes
- Sacred groves, ceremonial sites, and cultural practices
- Age-set initiation systems and social organization
- Pastoral and agricultural economy

## Colonial Period (1890-1963)
- 1890s: European colonial establishment in Mount Kenya region
- 1904: Kikuyu Reserve established, limiting African land rights
- 1920s: Limited African cash crop cultivation permitted
- 1952-1960: [[../../Kikuyu/Mau Mau Uprising|Mau Mau]] uprising, Kirinyaga District participation
- 1960: State of Emergency lifted, transition to independence

## Post-Independence Period (1964-1989)
- 1964: Kenya independence, Mau Mau leaders gain political power
- 1965-1975: Land redistribution schemes in Kirinyaga
- 1970s: Coffee cooperative expansion and modernization
- 1980s: Tea cultivation expansion in highland zones

## Modern Development Period (1990-2012)
- 1990s: Market liberalization affecting agricultural marketing
- 1998: Coffee prices collapse, economic hardship for farmers
- 2000s: Focus on agricultural productivity improvement
- 2005: Comprehensive Land Law and constitutional reforms debate
- 2010: New Kenyan Constitution with devolution provisions

## Devolution Era (2013-Present)
- 2013: Kirinyaga County government established
- 2014: County development initiatives launched
- 2015: Water development projects underway
- 2017-2018: National political upheaval and county governance impacts
- 2020: COVID-19 pandemic and economic disruptions
- 2022: County development plans reviewed and updated
- 2023: Climate change impacts increasingly apparent
- 2024: Infrastructure improvement projects expanded
- 2025: Development initiatives and agricultural productivity focus
- 2026: County continuity with ongoing development challenges and opportunities

## Agricultural Milestones
- 1960s: Coffee cooperative system formalized
- 1970s: Mwea irrigation scheme expansion
- 1980s: Tea production technology improvements
- 1990s: Market liberalization of agricultural products
- 2000s: Quality improvement focus
- 2010s: Value addition initiatives and export orientation
- 2020s: Climate adaptation and sustainable agriculture focus

## Infrastructure Development
- 1960s-1970s: Primary road network construction
- 1980s-1990s: Road maintenance and improvement
- 2000s: Electricity grid expansion
- 2010s: Water supply system improvements
- 2020s: Digital infrastructure expansion and connectivity improvements

## Environmental and Demographic Trends
- 1960s-1980s: Forest clearing for agriculture and settlement
- 1990s-2000s: Early conservation efforts
- 2010s: Protected area management and conservation focus
- 2020s: Climate change adaptation and ecosystem management
