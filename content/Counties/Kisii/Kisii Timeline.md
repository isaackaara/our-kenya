This timeline traces major events in [[Kisii County.md|Kisii County's]] history from pre-colonial times through 2026, documenting the development of the region's economy, governance, and society.

## Pre-Colonial Era (Before 1890s)

- [[Gusii People Kisii.md|Gusii]] people settle in the fertile western highlands of present-day Kenya
- Development of traditional social structures and governance systems
- Agricultural production supporting growing populations
- Trade networks with neighboring regions
- Establishment of cultural practices and traditions

## Colonial Period (1890s-1963)

- 1890s: British colonial administration begins in the region
- 1900s: Colonial pacification campaigns and settlement
- 1920s-1930s: Introduction and expansion of [[Kisii Tea Industry.md|tea]] cultivation
- 1920s: [[Kisii Soapstone.md|Soapstone carving]] tradition begins development
- 1940s-1950s: Expansion of colonial administration and services
- 1950s-1960s: Nationalist movement and independence advocacy

## Early Post-Independence (1963-1980s)

- 1963: Kisii District governance under national government
- 1960s-1970s: Tea industry expansion and KTDA development
- 1970s: Agricultural development initiatives
- 1980s: Increased urbanization of Kisii town
- 1980s: Development of regional commercial centers

## 1990s

- 1990s: Multi-party democracy introduction in Kenya
- 1990s: Kisii economic growth and market expansion
- 1990s: Soapstone carving industry growth and export development
- 1990s: University education expansion
- 1990s: NGO proliferation and development activity

## 2000s

- 2000s: Continued economic development
- 2005: Land reforms and property registration programs
- 2008: Post-election violence impacts western Kenya region
- 2008: Reconciliation and peace-building efforts
- 2009: Constitutional referendum and governance changes

## 2010-2013: Constitutional Transition

- 2010: New Constitution approved and devolution framework established
- 2013: First county elections held
- 2013: Kisii County officially established as constitutional entity
- 2013: Transition from district to county government
- 2013: County governor and assembly begin operations

## 2013-2018: First County Government Term

- 2013: County development planning begins
- 2014: Infrastructure projects initiated
- 2015: County education expansion programs
- 2016: Healthcare system expansion
- 2017: Political campaigns for 2017 elections
- 2018: Second county elections held

## 2018-2022: Second County Government Term

- 2018: New county governor takes office
- 2018-2022: Infrastructure development projects
- 2019: National census conducted
- 2020: COVID-19 pandemic impacts county
- 2021: Economic recovery from pandemic
- 2022: Third county elections held

## 2022-Present (2022-2026)

- 2022: Third county government established
- 2023: County development planning continuation
- 2023: Tea industry challenges and adaptation
- 2024: Climate impacts on agriculture
- 2024: Technology and digital service expansion
- 2025: Continued devolution implementation
- 2026: Ongoing county development initiatives

## Key Economic Moments

- 1920s: Tea introduced to the region
- 1950s-1960s: Post-independence agricultural expansion
- 1980s-1990s: Soapstone export market development
- 2000s: Dairy farming commercialization
- 2010s: County government fiscal decentralization

## Political Milestones

- 1960: Nationalist mobilization
- 1963: Independence achieved
- 1992: Multi-party politics introduced
- 2010: Constitutional reform approved
- 2013: County devolution begins

## Governance Changes

- 1890s-1963: Colonial administration
- 1963-2013: District governance under national structure
- 2013-present: County devolved governance

## Social Development

- 1960s-1980s: Primary school expansion
- 1980s-2000s: Secondary school growth
- 2000s-present: University and higher education development
- 1960s-present: Healthcare facility expansion
- 1990s-present: NGO emergence and growth

## Cultural and Social Events

- Ongoing: Preservation of initiation and marriage traditions
- 1970s-present: Soapstone carving industry maturation
- 1990s-present: Contemporary music development
- 2000s-present: Cultural organization and heritage preservation
