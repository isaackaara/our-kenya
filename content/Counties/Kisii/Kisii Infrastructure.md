[[Kisii County|Kisii County]] has developed substantial road and transportation infrastructure connecting the region to major Kenyan cities and neighboring counties. Infrastructure development remains ongoing as the county government prioritizes connectivity and service delivery.

## Road Networks

Primary roads include:
- The main highway linking Kisii to Nairobi and other major cities
- County roads connecting Kisii town to sub-county centers
- Rural roads providing access to markets and agricultural zones
- Recently upgraded routes improving connectivity to neighboring counties
- Ongoing road rehabilitation and maintenance programs

## Challenges

Despite improvements, road infrastructure faces challenges including:
- Deterioration during heavy rainfall seasons
- Limited maintenance budgets for rural roads
- Traffic congestion in Kisii town during market days
- Safety concerns on some rural routes
- Need for bridges and drainage improvements

## Transportation Services

Kisii town serves as a regional transportation hub with:
- Bus and matatu services connecting to Nairobi and other cities
- Local transportation networks within the county
- Commercial trading centers requiring transport infrastructure
- Regular routes to neighboring counties and regions

## Water and Sanitation

County government has expanded water supply systems:
- Piped water systems in urban areas and larger towns
- Community water schemes in rural areas
- Challenges providing water during dry seasons
- Sanitation facilities in schools and public institutions
- Ongoing programs expanding household water access

## Communication

Modern communication infrastructure includes:
- Mobile phone networks serving most populated areas
- Internet connectivity in towns and urban centers
- Radio broadcast systems
- Limited broadband penetration in rural areas

## Healthcare Facilities

Medical infrastructure includes:
- Kisii Teaching and Referral Hospital (county's main health facility)
- Health centers and dispensaries across sub-counties
- Equipment and staffing challenges in some facilities
