Key dates and events in Baringo County's history from pre-colonial times through 2026.

## Pre-Colonial and Early Colonial Period

1880s-1890s: Tugen pastoral territories encompass areas of central Kenya including present-day Baringo. Colonial contact occurs through traders and explorers.

1900s-1920s: British colonial administration extends formal authority over Baringo. District governance is established with Kabarnet as administrative center.

1920s-1950s: Colonial administration imposes taxation systems and governance structures on pastoral communities. Christian missions establish education and health facilities.

## Post-Independence and Development Period

1964: Kenya achieves independence. Baringo becomes a district within Rift Valley Province under national government administration.

1968-1970: Group ranches are established in Baringo converting communal pastoral lands to collective landholdings.

1978: Daniel arap Moi becomes President. Moi's presidency initiates substantial government resource allocation toward Baringo County.

1980s: Group ranch land begins subdivision and conversion to individual title. Land sales to external investors accelerate.

## Tourism Development Period

1990s-2000s: Lake Baringo tourism infrastructure develops with Island Camp and other facilities. Tourism becomes a growing economic sector.

## Contemporary Period

2000: Severe drought affects pastoral areas causing substantial livestock losses.

2010: Kenya adopts new Constitution establishing devolved county governance systems.

2011: Severe drought affects pastoral populations. Food security crisis occurs in Baringo and surrounding pastoral counties.

2013: Baringo County government is established as a constitutional entity with elected governor and county assembly.

2016-2017: Severe drought affects pastoral and agricultural areas with significant humanitarian impacts.

2018-2026: Lake Baringo water level rises dramatically, flooding shoreline infrastructure. Continued pastoral livelihood pressures and tourism development characterize contemporary dynamics.

2022: County gubernatorial and assembly elections determine county leadership.
