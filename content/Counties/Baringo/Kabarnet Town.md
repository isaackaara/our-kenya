Kabarnet serves as the county headquarters and principal urban center in Baringo County, located in the Tugen Hills at elevation approximately 1,950 meters. The town functions as the commercial and administrative hub for county government and surrounding pastoral and agricultural communities.

Kabarnet has experienced gradual urbanization reflecting increased commercial activity, government administration, and service expansion. The town's climate and elevation make it an attractive location for agriculture and habitation.

## Geographic Setting

Kabarnet is located on the eastern escarpment of the Rift Valley in the Tugen Hills. The elevation provides moderate climate temperatures compared to lower Rift Valley areas.

The town is connected to other regions through roads extending to Nairobi, Lake Baringo, and surrounding pastoral areas.

## Economic Functions

Kabarnet's economy centers on government services, commerce, agriculture, and services supporting surrounding pastoral and agricultural communities. The town serves as a market center for agricultural produce and pastoral livestock.

Hotels, restaurants, and service facilities accommodate travelers and commerce participants.

## Infrastructure and Services

The town has government offices, health facilities, banks, and utility infrastructure supporting urban functions. Educational institutions including secondary schools serve surrounding communities.
