Young people in Laikipia County face limited opportunities in an economy dominated by ranching, conservation, and pastoralism. Youth unemployment is elevated. Pastoral youth face particular challenges as herding becomes economically marginal. Urban migration draws youth seeking economic opportunity. Education investments seek non-traditional employment. Youth organizations provide platforms for collective action. Youth engagement in conservation and tourism provides some employment.

## Agricultural and Pastoral Employment

Ranch employment provides some youth jobs. However, opportunities are limited. Pastoral youth face declining viability of herding. Some youth maintain pastoral traditions despite challenges. Others abandon pastoralism for alternative livelihoods.

## Conservation and Tourism Employment

Conservancy and tourism employment attracts some youth. Guiding, security, and service work provide income. Employment in conservation provides regular wages. However, employment numbers are limited relative to youth population.

## Urban Migration

Many youth migrate to Nairobi and other cities. Urban migration seeks employment opportunities. Economic ties between rural and urban youth remain. Remittances support rural families. However, migration causes social disruption.

## Education and Skills

Youth invest in education seeking employment. Secondary and tertiary education remain aspirations. Technical and vocational training provide skills. However, employment prospects remain limited. Many educated youth struggle to find work.

## Youth Organizations

Youth groups organize around shared interests. Saving groups provide economic platforms. Youth political organizations mobilize voters. Religious organizations provide support. However, youth organizations have limited institutional strength.

## Business and Self-Employment

Some youth establish small businesses. Microfinance programs support entrepreneurship. Business success varies widely. Many businesses remain marginal.

## Social Challenges

Youth face pressures related to substance abuse and crime. Violence affects some youth. Gender-based violence victimizes young women. Mental health challenges emerge from economic stress. Support services remain limited.
