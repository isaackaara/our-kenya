# Laikipia Infrastructure

Infrastructure in Laikipia County serves the dispersed population and economic activities. Nanyuki airport provides air access. Road networks connect towns though many feeder roads remain unpaved. Electricity reaches most settlements. Water supply systems support urban and some rural areas. Telecommunications provide mobile phone coverage. Healthcare and education facilities serve communities. Infrastructure development has been uneven across the county.

## Transportation

Nanyuki airport provides regional air access. Roads connect Nanyuki to Nairobi and other towns. Secondary roads branch to ranch and community areas. Feeder roads remain unpaved and inaccessible during rains. Road maintenance is inadequate in many areas.

## Electricity

The national power grid extends through much of the county. Towns have reliable electricity supply. Rural areas may experience intermittent supply. Diesel generators supplement grid electricity in some locations.

## Water Supply

Water sources include rivers and boreholes. Water supply systems exist in towns and some areas. Water scarcity occurs in semi-arid zones. Water rights are contested. Water management remains inadequate.

## Healthcare Facilities

Nanyuki has county hospital providing tertiary care. Health centers and dispensaries provide basic services. Healthcare quality varies by location. Medicine shortages affect service delivery.

## Educational Facilities

Primary and secondary schools serve communities. Education quality varies between schools. Technical and vocational training exists in limited form.

## Communication Networks

Mobile phone networks provide coverage to most areas. Internet connectivity has expanded. Digital financial services enable mobile money transfer.
