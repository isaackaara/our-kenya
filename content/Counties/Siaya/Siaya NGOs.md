# Siaya NGOs

Non-governmental organizations work extensively in [[Siaya County]], addressing priority development issues including HIV prevention and treatment, health service provision, education support, and economic empowerment. NGOs supplement government services and implement targeted interventions.

## HIV and AIDS Organizations

Multiple HIV-focused NGOs operate in Siaya, providing testing services, antiretroviral therapy support, prevention education, and care services. Organizations work with key populations including [[Siaya Fishing Communities|fishing communities]] and implement community mobilization around HIV issues.

## Health Service Organizations

Health-focused organizations provide maternal health services, immunization support, and health education. Some organizations support county health facilities with equipment and supplies. Community health worker programs employ volunteers in health promotion.

## Education Support Organizations

NGOs support education through scholarship programs, school feeding initiatives, and education advocacy. Some organizations construct school facilities and provide teaching and learning materials.

## Water and Sanitation Organizations

Water and sanitation organizations construct water facilities, promote hygiene practices, and support water point maintenance. These efforts address critical water access and sanitation challenges.

## Economic Empowerment Organizations

Microfinance organizations provide credit and savings services to individuals and groups. Agricultural extension organizations provide training and market linkages. Women's and youth economic empowerment programs address unemployment.

## Conservation and Environmental Organizations

Environmental organizations work on water conservation, wetland protection, and sustainable resource management. [[../../Conservation/Lake Victoria Kenya|Lake Victoria]] conservation efforts engage fishing communities in conservation practices.

## Governance and Rights Organizations

Human rights and governance organizations work on community advocacy, rights awareness, and governance accountability. Organizations engage in community mobilization and advocacy for service improvement.

## Coordination and Partnerships

NGOs work with county government, national government agencies, and community organizations in implementing programs. Coordination forums facilitate information sharing and partnership development.
