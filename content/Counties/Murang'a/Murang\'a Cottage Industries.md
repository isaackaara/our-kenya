# Murang'a Cottage Industries

Small-scale manufacturing and crafts provide livelihoods in rural Murang'a County. Charcoal production, though environmentally problematic, provides income for some households. Carpentry and woodwork support local construction and furniture making. Brick making supplies construction materials. Pottery and basket weaving continue as traditional crafts. These cottage industries complement agricultural income and provide employment, though value added remains limited by technology and market access constraints.
