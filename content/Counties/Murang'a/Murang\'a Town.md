# Murang'a Town

Murang'a Town (formerly Fort Hall) is the county headquarters and main commercial center. Located at approximately 1,700 meters elevation, the town serves as an agricultural service hub and regional market center. Government offices, hospitals, schools, and trading posts concentrate in the town. The town functions as a transit point on the Nairobi to [[../../Conservation/Mount Kenya National Park|Mount Kenya]] corridor.
