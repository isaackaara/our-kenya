# Murang'a Beekeeping

Beekeeping is a significant livelihood in Murang'a County, with both traditional and modern hive systems in use. Diverse vegetation across elevation zones provides year-round nectar and pollen sources. Honey production supplements household incomes and provides products for domestic and international markets. Beekeepers organize in associations to improve market linkages. Processing capacity remains limited, constraining value capture. Beekeeping provides income with minimal land use.
