# Murang'a Notable People

Murang'a County has produced individuals achieving prominence in national politics, business, and civil society. Political leaders from Murang'a have served as governors, MPs, and cabinet ministers. Business and professional figures have contributed to Kenya's private sector. Civil society leaders have worked on community development issues. Educational and religious leaders have shaped institutional life. Athletes and cultural figures have achieved regional and national recognition.
