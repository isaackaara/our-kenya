# Murang'a and the Mau Mau

Murang'a District was deeply involved in the Mau Mau uprising (1952-1960). Oathing ceremonies mobilized Kikuyu communities for anti-colonial struggle. Mau Mau fighters operated from forests above Murang'a. The British counterinsurgency involved military operations, detention camps, and civilian restrictions. Murang'a communities suffered loss of life and economic disruption. The uprising's trauma left lasting impacts on Murang'a society and consciousness.
