# Murang'a Women

Women play crucial roles in Murang'a County's agricultural economy, managing household food production and participating in cash crop farming. Women's participation in dairy production and cooperatives has expanded. Land rights for women have been strengthened through legal reform, though implementation remains incomplete. Women's groups provide social solidarity and economic opportunity. Women's leadership in community organizations and government has increased through devolution structures.
