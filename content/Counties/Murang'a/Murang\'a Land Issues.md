# Murang'a Land Issues

Land tenure in Murang'a involves individual titles and smallhold fragmentation. Progressive subdivision through inheritance has created small, economically marginal holdings for many farmers. Land disputes over boundaries and inheritance rights are common. Women's land rights have improved through legal reform but face implementation challenges. Rising land values in areas near Nairobi have increased speculation. Land market activity is significant.
