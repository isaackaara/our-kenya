# Murang'a Dairy

Dairy farming integrates with crop production across Murang'a County. Most farmers maintain 1 to 5 dairy cows. Milk is sold to cooperative collection centers supplying large processors. Dairy provides regular household income supplementing seasonal cash crop returns. Dairy cooperatives offer input services and animal health support. Cattle manure improves soil fertility. Dairy productivity depends on adequate pasture and fodder supply.
