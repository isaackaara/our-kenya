# Murang'a Kikuyu Identity

Murang'a is a core Kikuyu county, with Kikuyu culture and identity central to the region. The Kikuyu language is widely spoken. Traditional practices including initiation ceremonies (though modified) continue. Kikuyu religious beliefs blend Christianity with traditional reverence for [[../../Conservation/Mount Kenya National Park|Mount Kenya]]. Political consciousness emphasizes Kikuyu community interests. Educational attainment is relatively high, supporting professional and entrepreneurial advancement.
