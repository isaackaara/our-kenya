# Murang'a Youth

Murang'a youth face unemployment and underemployment challenges despite educational attainment. Agricultural cooperative succession presents opportunities for youth engagement in farming, but capital and land constraints limit entry. Urban migration to Nairobi continues as youth seek employment opportunities. The coffee cooperative succession challenge (with aging membership) creates potential opportunities for youth assuming leadership roles.
