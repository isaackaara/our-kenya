# Murang'a Climate

Murang'a County experiences highland climate with temperatures ranging from 10 to 20 degrees Celsius. Rainfall is substantial, ranging from 1,000 to 2,000 millimeters annually. Two rainy seasons (March to May and October to November) support agricultural production. The cool, moist climate creates conditions favorable for coffee, tea, and dairy production. Water resources are abundant in higher elevations.
