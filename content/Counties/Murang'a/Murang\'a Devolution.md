# Murang'a Devolution

Murang'a County government was established in 2013 with elected governor and county assembly. County development initiatives have focused on agricultural support, infrastructure improvement, and service delivery expansion. County governance quality varies, with ongoing capacity challenges. Devolution has decentralized service delivery, potentially improving local responsiveness. Funding constraints limit development ambitions. Inter-county coordination occurs on shared resources.
