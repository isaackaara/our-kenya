# Murang'a Education

Murang'a County has a strong educational tradition with secondary schools distributed throughout the county. Primary schools provide foundational education. Higher education access has expanded through county colleges and university campuses. Educational attainment is relatively high compared to national averages. Educational achievement supports professional and business careers. Mission schools established in the colonial period provided early educational access.
