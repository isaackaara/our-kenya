# Murang'a Irrigation

Irrigation schemes utilize water from the Tana River and other water sources to extend agricultural production beyond rainfed farming. Irrigation enables cultivation of high-value horticultural crops including vegetables and fruits. Community-managed irrigation schemes operate with varying degrees of success and sustainability. Irrigation development requires infrastructure investment and water allocation management. Conflicts sometimes arise between different water uses during dry seasons.
