# Murang'a Tea

Tea cultivation occurs in higher-altitude portions of Murang'a County where rainfall and temperature favor tea growth. Tea provides steadier income than coffee due to year-round harvesting. Cooperatives manage tea collection and marketing. Processing in cooperative factories ensures quality. Both smallholders and estate farms produce tea. Export markets absorb production alongside domestic consumption.
