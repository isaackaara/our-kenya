# Murang'a Agriculture

Murang'a County's agricultural economy centers on coffee, tea, dairy, banana, and pineapple (particularly the Thika pineapple). Diversified production balances cash crop income with household food security. Agroforestry integrates trees with crops. Farmers maintain livestock alongside crop production. Cooperatives organize marketing for most commodities. Irrigation schemes expand water-assured agriculture. Agricultural productivity remains high due to favorable climate and soil quality.
