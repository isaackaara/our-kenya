# Murang'a Health

Healthcare in Murang'a is provided through government health facilities ranging from health centers to the county referral hospital. Healthcare access is better in towns than in remote areas. Maternal and child health programs receive priority. Disease control programs address malaria, HIV/AIDS, and tuberculosis. Health worker availability and facility equipment remain limiting factors in some areas. Healthcare outcomes are generally better than national averages due to relative prosperity.
