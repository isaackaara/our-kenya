# Murang'a and Nairobi

Murang'a County functions as a bedroom county for Nairobi workers, with workers commuting daily or living part-time in Murang'a while working in the capital. The proximity to Nairobi has attracted residential development and real estate investment near towns. Murang'a supplies agricultural products (vegetables, dairy, fruits) to Nairobi markets. The relationship creates economic interdependence and rapid urbanization pressures. Infrastructure connecting Murang'a to Nairobi is critical.
