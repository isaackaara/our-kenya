# Murang'a Infrastructure

Murang'a County's road network includes the main Nairobi to [[../../Conservation/Mount Kenya National Park|Mount Kenya]] highway running through the county. Secondary roads connect market centers and constituencies. River crossings require bridges maintaining access between areas. Power supply reaches most urban areas and many rural locations. Water supply systems serve towns, though rural access remains inadequate. Improved infrastructure has facilitated development and reduced transport costs.
