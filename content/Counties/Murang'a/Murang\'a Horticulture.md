# Murang'a Horticulture

Murang'a County has developed horticultural sectors including cut flowers and vegetable production for export and domestic markets. Horticultural crops provide higher per-hectare returns than traditional crops. Export-oriented production operates under contracts with exporters. Infrastructure, capital, and technical knowledge are required for successful horticulture. Horticultural development has created employment and new income opportunities for farmers.
