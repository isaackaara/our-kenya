# Murang'a Coffee

Coffee is a major cash crop in Murang'a County, cultivated on mid-altitude slopes where conditions are favorable. Murang'a coffee competes with Nyeri and other highland origins in quality. Cooperatives organize farmer production and marketing. Coffee provides significant income for thousands of smallholder farmers. Quality varies, with premium grades commanding higher prices. Processing through cooperative wet mills ensures quality standards. Export markets absorb most production.
