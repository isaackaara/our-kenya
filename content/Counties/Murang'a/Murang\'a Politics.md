# Murang'a Politics

Murang'a County politics reflect GEMA alignment and Kikuyu community interests. County-level governance since 2013 involves competitive elections for governor and assembly positions. Parliamentary constituencies elect Members of Parliament. Political dynamics involve multiple factions and leadership competition. Educational attainment supports politically engaged population with governance demands. Civil society organizations monitor county performance.
