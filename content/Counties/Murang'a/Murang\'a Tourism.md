# Murang'a Tourism

Tourism in Murang'a County is developing, with attractions including the Aberdare foothills and tea and coffee country. Agricultural tourism, particularly coffee and tea farm visits, presents opportunities. Cultural tourism potential includes Kikuyu heritage experiences. Accommodation infrastructure is limited but expanding. Tourism development remains less advanced than in major national parks but growth opportunities exist.
