# Murang'a Sacred Sites

Murang'a County contains sacred hills and sites important in Kikuyu cosmology. [[../../Conservation/Mount Kenya National Park|Mount Kenya]] itself holds spiritual significance as the traditional dwelling of Ngai (God). Sacred groves protected as dwelling places of spiritual presences face degradation from development pressures. Burial grounds and cemetery sites maintain ancestral significance. Sites associated with important historical events hold cultural value. Cultural heritage protection efforts attempt to preserve sites against urbanization and development.
