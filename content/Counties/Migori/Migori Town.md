# Migori Town

Migori town is the administrative capital and principal commercial center of Migori County. Located in the interior of the county, the town serves as the hub for government activity, commerce, and service provision.

## Location and Function

Migori town is situated inland from Lake Victoria shore, connected to the border region, Kisii County, and other regions by road networks. The town serves as the primary market center for surrounding agricultural and mining communities. It is the seat of county government.

## Government Center

The county government headquarters are located in Migori town, housing the governor's office, county assembly, and government departments. County administrative functions concentrate in the town center.

## Commerce and Markets

The town center contains markets, shops, and trading establishments serving local and regional populations. Market activities center on agricultural products, mining-related goods, and general merchandise. Weekly market days draw traders from surrounding areas.

## Services

Migori town provides banking, telecommunications, healthcare, and educational services. Government offices, courts, and other institutions are located in the town. Hotels and restaurants serve visitors and residents.

## Mining Connection

Gold mining activity in the county influences the town economy, with miners purchasing supplies and services in town. Gold trading and related services operate in commercial areas.

## Development

Migori town has experienced modest growth with improved road connectivity. Town development remains constrained by limited investment and provincial economy scale. The town continues as the principal commercial center.
