## Key Dates in Migori County History

**15th-17th Centuries:** Luo migration into Lake Victoria region. Settlement of Migori area by Luo-speaking peoples.

**1890s:** British colonial penetration into Nyanza region begins. Colonial administrative posts established.

**1903:** Formal colonial administration of Migori area established under British colonial governance.

**1930-1960:** Colonial period governance. Economic transformation through mining and agriculture development.

**1963:** Kenya independence declared. Nyanza Province governance continues.

**1992:** Kenya transitions to multi-party democracy. Migori participates in competitive elections.

**2002:** Democratic consolidation. Migori remains politically engaged.

**2010:** Kenya's new constitution implemented. Devolution transfers power to county governments.

**2013:** First Migori County elections held. County government assumes service delivery responsibilities.

**2017:** Second county elections. Continued county governance and development.

**2022:** Fourth general election cycle. County government continues development initiatives.

**2024:** Mercury reduction initiatives advance in artisanal gold mining areas.

**2025:** Sony Sugar management transition occurs with new leadership.

**2026:** Present. Migori County continues implementing development programs addressing gold mining impacts, fishing sustainability, and service delivery improvement.
