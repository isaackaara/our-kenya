# Ol Kalou Town

Ol Kalou is the county headquarters of Nyandarua County, located in the cool highlands at approximately 2,400 meters elevation. The town serves as the administrative center for county government functions. Market activities center on agricultural trading, particularly wheat and dairy products. The highland location creates a distinctive cool climate in the town. Services including government offices, banks, schools, and health facilities serve surrounding communities.

## Administrative Functions

County government offices coordinate administrative functions for Nyandarua. The governor, county assembly, and county executive operate from Ol Kalou. Government employees conduct local governance. District-level administrators historically worked from Ol Kalou before devolution. The town's status as administrative center has supported its growth.

## Market Activities

Agricultural markets in Ol Kalou handle wheat, potatoes, dairy products, and other agricultural commodities. Farmers bring products to town markets to sell. Traders and wholesalers operate from market stalls and shops. Regular market days bring activity and commerce. Agricultural prices are influenced by supply and demand in Ol Kalou markets.

## Commercial Sector

Shops and trading centers serve local populations. Banks and financial institutions provide services. Petroleum dealers sell fuel. Hotels and restaurants serve travelers and residents. Commercial property has developed as the town economy has grown. Business owners in Ol Kalou engage in agricultural and service commerce.

## Education and Health

Secondary schools and primary schools operate in Ol Kalou. The county hospital provides healthcare services. Health centers provide basic healthcare. Educational and health services attract people from surrounding areas. Quality of services affects town population and economic activity.

## Climate and Setting

The high-altitude location creates cool temperatures and high rainfall. The natural environment is green and forested. The cold climate is distinctive compared to lower-altitude Kenyan towns. Agricultural productivity from the cool climate has supported the town's agricultural base.

## Infrastructure

Roads connect Ol Kalou to other towns and regions. Water supplies serve the town and surrounding areas. Electricity reaches the town. Telecommunications provide mobile phone coverage. Infrastructure development has expanded town facilities.

## Population and Growth

The town population has grown with agricultural development and administrative functions. Both residential and commercial expansion has occurred. Migration to the town reflects economic opportunities. The town remains a secondary market center rather than major urban area.
