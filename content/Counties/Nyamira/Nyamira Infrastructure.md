Nyamira County infrastructure supports agricultural commerce, service delivery, and connectivity. Road networks, water systems, and utilities connect the county to regional and national markets.

## Road Networks

Roads include:
- County roads connecting subcounties
- Rural roads to farming areas
- Main highway connections
- Market access routes
- Ongoing maintenance programs

## Transportation

Transportation services include:
- Matatu and bus services
- Taxi operations within county
- Connectivity to Kisii and Bomet
- Market transportation
- Cargo logistics

## Water Systems

Water infrastructure includes:
- Urban piped water supply
- Rural community water schemes
- Water points and boreholes
- Storage and distribution systems
- Seasonal water challenges

## Sanitation

Sanitation facilities include:
- Public toilets in towns
- Household latrine promotion
- Drainage systems
- Waste management
- Health facility sanitation

## Electricity

Power supply includes:
- Grid electricity access
- Limited rural electrification
- Solar power growing
- Transformer and distribution infrastructure
- Supply reliability challenges

## Communication

Communication infrastructure includes:
- Mobile phone networks
- Internet connectivity in towns
- Radio broadcast services
- Limited broadband coverage
- Digital service expansion

## Healthcare Facilities

Medical facilities include:
- County hospitals
- Health centers and dispensaries
- Community health workers
- Pharmaceutical services
