This timeline documents major events in Nyamira County history from its creation in 2013 through 2026, tracing governance development, economic progress, and social change.

## Pre-County Era (Before 2013)

- Centuries: Gusii and Kipsigis settlement and cultural development
- 1890s-1963: British colonial administration
- 1920s-1930s: Tea cultivation introduction
- 1963-2013: Kisii District governance
- 1963-2013: Post-independence agricultural expansion
- 2000s: Development organization growth
- 2010: New Constitution adoption with devolution framework

## County Creation (2010-2013)

- 2010: Constitution approved establishing devolution
- 2011-2012: Devolution preparation and transition planning
- 2013: Nyamira County officially established
- March 2013: First county elections held
- August 2013: County government begins operations

## First County Government (2013-2018)

- 2013: County development planning begins
- 2014: Infrastructure project initiation
- 2015: Healthcare facility expansion
- 2016: Educational infrastructure development
- 2017: Electoral campaigns and political competition
- 2018: Second county elections held

## Second County Government (2018-2022)

- 2018: New county governor takes office
- 2018-2022: Continuous infrastructure projects
- 2019: National census conducted
- 2020: COVID-19 pandemic impacts services
- 2021: Economic recovery efforts
- 2022: Third county elections held

## Third County Government (2022-Present)

- 2022: Third county governor administration begins
- 2023: County development plan updates
- 2023: Climate adaptation programs
- 2024: Technology service expansion
- 2024: Agricultural productivity initiatives
- 2025: Devolution implementation continuation
- 2026: Ongoing county development

## Economic Developments

- 1920s: Tea introduction and early cultivation
- 1970s-1980s: Tea industry expansion
- 2000s: Dairy farming commercialization
- 2010s-present: Value addition and agricultural diversification
- 2020s: Climate adaptation focus

## Infrastructure Milestones

- 2014: Road rehabilitation programs
- 2015: Water supply expansion
- 2018: Energy infrastructure improvement
- 2020: Telecommunications expansion
- 2023: Transportation network improvement

## Social Development

- 1960s-1980s: School expansion
- 1990s-2000s: Secondary education growth
- 2000s-present: Higher education access
- 2010s-present: Healthcare service expansion
- 2020s: Digital literacy and technology

## Agricultural Evolution

- 1920s: Tea introduction begins
- 1960s-1980s: Cooperative system development
- 1990s: Agricultural intensification
- 2000s: Dairy growth begins
- 2010s: Value addition initiatives
- 2020s: Climate-smart agriculture focus

## Governance Changes

- 2013: District system transforms to county governance
- 2013: Democratic elections introduced for governor
- 2015: Women representation in government
- 2018: Electoral system adjustments
- 2022: Third election cycle

## Political Events

- 2013: First gubernatorial campaigns
- 2018: Second electoral competition
- 2022: Third electoral cycle
- 2020: Inter-ethnic cohesion initiatives
- 2023: Governance accountability focus

## Cultural Preservation

- 2010s: Heritage documentation begins
- 2015: Cultural organization strengthening
- 2018: Youth engagement in traditions
- 2020s: Technology-assisted culture preservation

## Climate and Environment

- 2015: Climate adaptation programs begin
- 2018: Water conservation initiatives
- 2020: Climate information services
- 2023: Green energy projects
- 2025: Resilience program expansion
