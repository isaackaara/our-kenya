Nyamira County governance has operated under Kenya's devolution framework since 2013, managing county functions and delivering services to residents. Devolution has decentralized governance and enabled localized service delivery and development planning.

## Constitutional Framework

Devolution operates through:
- County government structure
- Elected governor and county assembly
- County legislative authority
- Fiscal devolution of resources
- Service delivery responsibilities
- Democratic accountability mechanisms

## County Government Structure

County governance includes:
- Office of the Governor
- County assembly with 30+ members
- County executive and departments
- Sub-county administrations
- Ward-level governance
- Civil service workforce

## Devolved Functions

County responsibilities include:
- Primary and secondary education
- Primary healthcare services
- Water and sanitation services
- County road infrastructure
- Agriculture extension services
- Local government functions

## Revenue and Finance

County fiscal management involves:
- Equitable share of national revenue
- Conditional and unconditional grants
- Local revenue collection
- County budget preparation
- Financial accountability
- Development project funding

## Service Delivery

County government provides:
- Healthcare services
- Educational services
- Water supply management
- Infrastructure maintenance
- Extension services
- Community development support

## Governance Challenges

Devolution implementation faces:
- Capacity constraints
- Corruption and accountability concerns
- Political interference in service delivery
- Coordination with national government
- Resource adequacy
- Community participation gaps

## Development Planning

County planning involves:
- Five-year development plans
- Project identification and prioritization
- Community consultation processes
- Implementation coordination
- Monitoring and evaluation
- Plan revision and updating

## Citizen Participation

Community engagement includes:
- Public hearings and consultations
- Ward development committees
- Community organization participation
- Feedback mechanisms
- Accountability forums
- Electoral participation

## Future Directions

Devolution development priorities:
- Institutional capacity strengthening
- Financial management improvement
- Service delivery expansion
- Corruption reduction
- Community participation enhancement
- Sustainable development advancement
