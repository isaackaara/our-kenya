Nyamira County was created in 2013 as part of Kenya's constitutional devolution process following the adoption of the 2010 Constitution. The county was carved from the former Kisii District, establishing a separate governance entity with its own elected government.

## Constitutional Context

Nyamira's creation resulted from:
- 2010 Constitution's devolution framework
- Transition from district to county governance
- Decentralization of government functions
- Transfer of service delivery responsibilities
- Establishment of county democratic institutions
- Fiscal devolution to county governments

## Carving from Kisii District

The creation process involved:
- Division of former Kisii District into Kisii and Nyamira counties
- Boundary demarcation and definition
- Population distribution between counties
- Asset and resource allocation
- Staff transfer and institutional restructuring
- Historical administrative unit transformation

## First County Elections (2013)

The inaugural county elections:
- Held in 2013 following constitutional framework
- Selected county governor and assembly members
- Established new county government institutions
- Began decentralized governance operations
- Created county-level democratic accountability
- Initiated devolution implementation

## Transition Challenges

The transition faced obstacles:
- Administrative structure establishment
- Institutional capacity development
- Service delivery continuity maintenance
- Asset allocation resolution
- Staff transitions and recruitment
- Budget and financial management systems

## Impact on the Region

County creation affected:
- Political dynamics and competition
- Governance structure and representation
- Ethnic identity and political organization
- Service delivery localization
- Resource allocation and prioritization
- Community participation opportunities

## Governance Since 2013

Since creation, Nyamira County has:
- Developed county-specific policies
- Implemented development programs
- Managed county finances and budgets
- Provided services (health, education, water)
- Built county institutions and capacity
- Engaged communities in development

## Comparative Position

Nyamira County relative to Kisii:
- Smaller population
- Comparable population density
- Similar tea-based economy
- Distinct political entity
- Separate governance structures
- Different development priorities

## Constitutional Framework

County governance operates under:
- County government devolved functions
- National government retained functions
- Fiscal devolution provisions
- Democratic accountability mechanisms
- County assembly legislative power
- County service delivery role
