# Makueni Mango Value Chain

Makueni County has developed mango production and processing as a development priority, establishing a county-level mango processing plant as a flagship economic development project. The mango value chain exemplifies agricultural commercialization and value addition strategies in semi-arid contexts.

Mango cultivation in Makueni occurs on diverse landholdings, from smallholder farmers producing mangoes as part of diversified agriculture to larger commercial orchards. Smallholder farmers often interplant mangoes with annual crops, using mangoes as long-term wealth accumulation. Commercial producers operate specialized orchards with attention to variety selection, tree management, and production optimization.

The county government established a mango processing plant as an infrastructure investment designed to add value to raw mangoes. The processing facility transforms fresh mangoes into dried products, juices, jams, and other processed goods with higher market value and longer shelf life. Processing enables year-round sales and access to distant markets rather than dependence on fresh fruit seasonal markets.

The mango value chain connects smallholder farmers through collection centres to the processing plant. Farmers sell fresh mangoes to traders and collection points, which accumulate mangoes for transport to the processing plant. The plant processes fruits and markets processed products through wholesale and retail channels.

Value distribution across the mango value chain has been contested, with farmers often capturing smaller portions of final consumer value. The processing plant aims to improve farmer returns by providing assured markets and potentially better prices. However, farmer organization, market information access, and value chain governance remain challenging.

Market development for processed mango products faces competition from other suppliers and limited domestic demand for value-added agricultural products. International market access requires compliance with food safety standards and shipping requirements. Export market development would enhance profitability and farmer benefits.

The mango processing initiative demonstrates county government investment in agricultural development, though long-term sustainability and profitability remain uncertain.
