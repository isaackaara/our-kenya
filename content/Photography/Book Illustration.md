Book illustration in Kenya encompasses artistic work created for publications including children's books, literary works, academic texts, and illustrated publications. Illustrators create artwork accompanying or enhancing written text, employing visual language to support, expand, or complement narrative and meaning. Professional book illustrators develop specialized skills in visual storytelling, character design, and composition. The development of book illustration as professional practice paralleled growth of publishing industries in Kenya. Publishers commissioned illustrations enhancing books and making them visually appealing to readers. Book illustration remains important practice within illustration fields and publishing industries.

Children's book illustration represents significant specialization within book illustration with distinct conventions and purposes. Children's book illustrators create engaging visual narratives supporting text for young readers, developing characters, settings, and visual styles appropriate to intended audiences. Quality children's book illustration can achieve high artistic standards while serving educational and entertainment functions. Many talented Kenyan illustrators have created children's books, some achieving regional or international recognition. Children's book illustration provided employment for illustrators while contributing to cultural production for youth. The development of children's literature industries has depended partly on availability of skilled illustrators.

Literary book illustration for novels and short story collections employs diverse visual approaches. Some illustrators create representational images depicting scenes or characters from texts. Others employ more abstract or interpretive approaches creating mood or enhancing thematic meanings. Literary illustration can achieve sophisticated artistic expression while serving supportive function to texts. Illustrated editions of literary works can be valuable cultural products combining excellent writing with excellent illustration. Contemporary literary illustrators create work across genres and literary traditions.

Academic and non-fiction book illustration serves explanatory and informational purposes requiring clarity and accuracy alongside visual appeal. Scientific illustration, botanical illustration, and technical illustration serve informational functions requiring precise visual communication. Educational textbooks employ illustrations clarifying and supporting text content. Information design uses illustration to convey complex information accessibly. Professional book illustrators develop expertise in varied illustration types serving different publishing purposes and audiences. Contemporary book illustration continues diversifying with new publishing formats, technological possibilities, and audience needs.
