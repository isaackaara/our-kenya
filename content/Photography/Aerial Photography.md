Aerial photography in Kenya documented landscape, urban, and environmental subjects from elevated perspectives using aircraft and elevation photography techniques. Photographers developed expertise in capturing aerial viewpoints, working from moving aircraft, and managing technical challenges of elevation photography. Photography of aerial work reveals the specialized equipment, piloting coordination, and technical knowledge required for aerial documentation. The visual archive of Kenyan aerial photography demonstrates photography's extension into vertical perspectives and its role in landscape and environmental documentation.

Landscape aerial photography captured Kenya's distinctive geography from elevated perspectives. Photographers documented the Rift Valley, Mount Kenya, national parks, and other geographic features from above. Photography of landscape documentation reveals dramatically different landscape perspectives possible from elevation. The visual record demonstrates aerial photography revealing landscape patterns and relationships invisible from ground level. Images of landscape work show landscape visible only from elevated vantage points. Landscape aerial documentation created permanent records of geographic features.

Urban and infrastructure aerial photography documented cities, roads, and development from elevated perspectives. Photographers captured urban layouts, infrastructure networks, and development patterns. Photography of urban documentation reveals development patterns visible from elevation. The visual archive demonstrates aerial photography serving urban documentation and development planning purposes. Images of urban work show urban environments' spatial organization. Urban aerial photography contributed to understanding urban development and planning.

Environmental and conservation aerial photography documented environmental conditions, land use change, and conservation areas. Photographers captured environmental transformation visible from elevation. Photography of environmental documentation reveals landscape change and environmental conditions. The visual record demonstrates aerial photography serving conservation and environmental documentation purposes. Images of environmental work show landscape transformation and conservation challenges. Environmental aerial photography created evidence of environmental change.

Agricultural and land use aerial photography documented farming systems, land allocation, and agricultural development. Photographers captured agricultural patterns and land use from elevated perspectives. Photography of agricultural documentation reveals agricultural systems and land utilization patterns. The visual archive demonstrates aerial photography serving agricultural documentation purposes. Images of agricultural work show farming systems' spatial organization. Agricultural photography contributed to understanding agricultural development.

Tourism and marketing aerial photography created promotional imagery presenting Kenya's attractions from aerial perspectives. Photographers produced scenic aerial imagery for tourism promotion. Images of promotional work show scenic Kenya from elevated vantage points. Tourism aerial photography supported Kenya's tourism marketing and promotion.
