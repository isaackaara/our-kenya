Botanical photography in Kenya documented plant life through specialized photographic practice emphasizing accurate representation of botanical subjects. Botanical photographers created imagery serving scientific documentation, educational purposes, and aesthetic appreciation of plant diversity. Photography of botanical practice reveals technical approaches, macro photography techniques, and the careful documentation required for botanical work. The visual archive of Kenyan botanical photography demonstrates photography's role in botanical science and plant documentation.

Scientific botanical photography created documentation for botanical institutions, research, and herbarium records. Photographers working in scientific contexts produced standardized documentation of plant specimens. Photography of scientific botanical work reveals technical standards and documentation protocols. The visual record demonstrates botanical photography serving scientific purposes. Images of specimen documentation show photography's role in biological knowledge creation. Botanical scientific documentation contributed to plant taxonomy and botanical research.

Agricultural photography closely related to botanical practice documented crop plants and agricultural systems. Photographers documented agricultural development, crop varieties, and farming practices. Photography of agricultural documentation reveals agricultural photography's role in development and modernization narratives. The visual archive demonstrates photography serving agricultural extension and farmer education. Images of agricultural photography show photography functioning in agricultural systems. Agricultural documentation served both marketing and educational purposes.

Ornamental and garden botanical photography emphasized aesthetically appealing plant subjects. Photographers captured flowers and plants in decorative and garden contexts. Photography of ornamental work reveals aesthetic approaches to botanical documentation. The visual record demonstrates botanical photography engaging with visual beauty alongside scientific documentation. Images of ornamental photography show photography celebrating plant aesthetics. Garden photography documented cultivated landscapes and horticultural practice.

Ethnobotanical photography documented traditional uses of plants and indigenous botanical knowledge. Photographers recorded plants important to traditional medicine, food systems, and cultural practices. Photography of ethnobotanical work reveals documentation of cultural botanical knowledge. The visual archive demonstrates botanical photography serving cultural documentation purposes. Images of ethnobotanical work show photography engaging with indigenous knowledge systems. Ethnobotanical documentation created records of traditional botanical knowledge.

Rare and endangered plant photography documented threatened botanical heritage. Photographers documented endangered plant species in natural habitats. Photography of conservation documentation reveals botanical photography's conservation potential. The visual record demonstrates botanical photography serving conservation purposes. Images of endangered plant documentation show photography contributing to conservation awareness. Botanical conservation photography created evidence of threatened plant species.
