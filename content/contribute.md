---
title: Suggest a Topic
description: Know a story Kenya should tell? Submit a topic suggestion for the Our Kenya knowledge vault.
---

Our Kenya grows through careful research and community suggestions. If you know a story that belongs here -- a county history, a political moment, a cultural tradition, a sporting legend -- tell us.

**What makes a good suggestion?** A specific topic (not "more about Kikuyu history"), a clear reason it matters, and ideally a source or two to point the researcher in the right direction.

Isaac reviews all suggestions. Approved topics are researched and written to Gold Standard before being added to the vault. You will not be credited by name unless you ask to be.
