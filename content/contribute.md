We don't have the whole story. Nobody does.

Our Kenya is a living archive, still growing. Every note that exists was researched and written because someone decided it mattered. Your suggestion might become the next one.

If you know a story that belongs here - a county history, a political moment, a cultural tradition, a sporting legend, a person who should be remembered - tell us. This is how the archive grows.

**What makes a good suggestion?** A specific topic, a clear reason it matters, and if you have them, a source or two. Not "more about Kikuyu history" - something like "The Kapenguria trial and why it made Kenyatta president before he was president."

Isaac reviews every suggestion. Approved topics are researched and written before being added to the vault. This takes time. It is done carefully.
