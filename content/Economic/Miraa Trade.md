# Miraa Trade

Miraa (khat) cultivation and trade represents an economically significant but socially contested sector of the Kenyan economy, particularly in northeastern and coastal regions.

## Production

Miraa is cultivated primarily in:
- Meru County: primary production region
- Coastal regions: secondary production
- Requires specific agro-climatic conditions

## Market and Trade

Miraa trade is highly profitable, generating significant income for producers and traders. Markets extend throughout East Africa and to diaspora communities internationally.

## Social and Health Concerns

Miraa consumption raises public health concerns:
- Stimulant effects
- Addictive properties
- Social impacts on users
- Controversial status in some regions

## Regulation

Miraa trade operates in a regulatory gray zone, with varying levels of prohibition and tolerance across Kenya's counties and in international markets.
