# 2010 Constitution

The Constitution of Kenya adopted in 2010 fundamentally restructured Kenya's governance system.

The 2010 Constitution introduced devolved government, separation of powers, and protected fundamental rights. Implementation required creation of county governments, reformed judiciary, and new legislative frameworks.

The constitution remains contested with debates about implementation and proposed amendments.
