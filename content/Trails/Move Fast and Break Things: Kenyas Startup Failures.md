For every Kenyan startup that made headlines with a funding round, another was quietly bleeding cash, faking traction, or collapsing under the weight of its own promises. The 2022-2024 funding winter exposed fault lines that had been papered over by easy venture capital. Sendy, Sky.Garden, Copia, Tulaa - each failure tells a different story about what went wrong in Silicon Savannah. This trail walks through the wreckage.
