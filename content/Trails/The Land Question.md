<p class="trail-intro-paragraph">Three questions hide inside The Land Question: who owns it now, who owned it then, and whether ownership is absolute or merely conditional on blood and memory. The British created the White Highlands by moving living people, the newly independent gave titles to fiction, and the disputes continue because the damage was deliberate. This trail explores how displacement created permanence and whether original sin can ever return to innocence.</p>
