title: "Silicon Savannah: From Cables to Unicorns"
---

In 2009, the TEAMS undersea cable landed at the Kenyan coast and everything changed. Bandwidth prices collapsed, a mobile revolution was already underway, and within a few years Nairobi had reinvented itself as Africa's leading tech hub. This trail traces the infrastructure, the ecosystem builders, and the capital flows that turned a city better known for safari tourism into the continent's startup capital.
