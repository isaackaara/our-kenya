<p class="trail-intro-paragraph">Raila Odinga has contested Kenya's presidential elections five times across fifteen years, each campaign a variation on the same impossible question: how to win when every victory returns to defeat. From the molasses plant to the handshake that never handshook, he has watched hope become bargaining and victory become negotiation. This trail follows the arc of ambition that learned to settle for accommodation, and the price of compromise when it becomes strategy.</p>
