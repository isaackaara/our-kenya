---
title: Lupita Nyong'o: The Global Luo
---

Lupita Nyong'o was born in Mexico, raised in Kenya, educated at Yale, and became the first Kenyan to win an Academy Award when she took Best Supporting Actress for 12 Years a Slave in 2014. She is Kisumu's daughter, Anyang' Nyong'o's child, a Luo woman who made Hollywood bend to her. She speaks about colorism, about representation, about what it means to be African in global spaces. She is what modern Kenya looks like when it travels: rooted, confident, and unapologetic.
