Maralal is [[Samburu County]] capital and largest urban center, serving as administrative hub, market center, and commercial focus. The town provides government services, banking, healthcare, and limited tourism services. Maralal's [[International Camel Derby]] held annually attracts tourists and generates economic activity. Urban population is predominantly [[Samburu]] though includes other ethnic groups.

## Urban Infrastructure and Services

Maralal has government offices, police station, hospital, schools, banks, and shops. The town has basic utilities (electricity, water) though service interruptions occur. Roads to Maralal have been improved, enabling better market access and commerce.

## Economic Activity

Market trading dominates Maralal economy, with agricultural produce and livestock marketing. Petty traders, restaurants, transport services, and shops provide commerce. Government and NGO employment provides wage income. Tourism services (hotels, restaurants, guides) supplement economy.

## International Camel Derby

The annual Camel Derby, held in September, has become Maralal's major tourist attraction. The event features camel racing and Samburu cultural demonstrations. Tourism revenue from the event provides substantial income to Maralal and surrounding areas.
