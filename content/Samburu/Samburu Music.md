[[Samburu]] musical traditions include [[The Moran (Warriors)|moran warrior songs]], women's blessing songs, and various ceremonial music forms. These traditions use distinctive instruments and vocal styles. Music plays important roles in social bonding, ritual, and entertainment. Contemporary music incorporates modern genres alongside traditional forms.

## Moran Warrior Songs

Moran songs celebrate warrior virtues, physical prowess, and pastoral values. These songs are performed by age-mate cohorts and function as social bonding mechanisms. Moran songs feature distinctive vocal styles and rhythmic patterns. Songs commemorate significant events and warrior achievements.

## Women's Blessing Songs

Women perform blessing songs at important occasions including marriages and celebrations. These songs provide spiritual blessing and community recognition. Women's vocal traditions emphasize group harmony and celebratory expression.

## Ceremonial Music

Circumcision ceremonies, marriage celebrations, and initiation rites feature specific musical forms. These musical traditions convey cultural knowledge and mark important transitions. Ceremonial music maintains cultural continuity and transmits values across generations.

## Traditional Instruments

Drums, horns, and string instruments provide musical accompaniment. Instruments are often handcrafted from available materials. Different instruments are associated with different musical genres and social contexts. Contemporary music increasingly uses manufactured instruments.

## Oral Transmission

Musical traditions are transmitted orally through performance and practice. Younger community members learn through participation in performances and celebrations. Language shift and modernization threaten oral transmission mechanisms.

## Contemporary Music Practice

Modern Samburu incorporate Kenyan popular music, gospel music, and global music genres. Young Samburu listen to recorded music through radio, phones, and internet. Some Samburu musicians create contemporary compositions blending traditional and modern elements.

## Cultural Preservation

Community and cultural organizations attempt to document and preserve [[Samburu]] musical traditions. Recording projects and performance events maintain visibility of traditional music. Schools sometimes include traditional music in cultural curricula.
